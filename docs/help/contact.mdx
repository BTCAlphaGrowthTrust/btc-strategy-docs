## What to include

To help us respond quickly, please mention:

- **Who you are** — the professional/institutional entity and your role.
- **What you need** — evaluation access, a license, a specific dataset question, or an
  integration issue.
- **Endpoint / details** — if it's a technical question, the endpoint and an example request
  help (see the [API Reference](/docs/api-reference)).

## Common topics

- **Access & licensing** — the access model is described in
  [Access & Authentication](/docs/get-started/access); license terms (DRAFT) are under
  [Licensing](/docs/legal/licensing).
- **Methodology** — most "how is X computed?" questions are answered in
  [Methodology](/docs/methodology/overview); if something there is unclear or looks off, tell us
  — it's written to be checked against the data.

:::note
This is a **data product** for professional/institutional use. Nothing we provide is investment
advice — see [Factual Data, Not Advice](/docs/legal/not-advice).
:::
