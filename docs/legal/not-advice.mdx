# Factual Data, Not Advice

This page states plainly what the data **is** and what it is **not**. It applies to every
endpoint, field and figure in this API, and is echoed in the `disclaimer` of every response
`meta`.

## What the data is

- **Factual historical data.** It reports **what a set of strategies did** in a **backtest** run
  over **public market data**.
- **Backtested / modelled.** Figures come from a bar-by-bar simulation, net of a documented
  [fee & slippage model](/docs/methodology/fees-slippage). Risk-rescaled figures are
  [modelled from the base backtest](/docs/methodology/risk-rescale), not re-simulated.
- **For professional / institutional use**, under a global data license
  ([Licensing](./licensing)).

## What the data is not

:::warning Not advice
- **Not investment advice** and **not a recommendation** to buy, sell, or hold anything.
- **Not a forecast or prediction.** Nothing here projects future performance.
- **Not an offer or solicitation**, and not a representation that any strategy is suitable for
  you.
- **Not a claim about live trading.** Backtested results are hypothetical; they do not reflect
  live execution, funding, market impact, or a specific live allocation
  ([Provenance](/docs/methodology/provenance)).
:::

## Past performance

**Past performance is not indicative of future results.** Backtested performance has inherent
limitations: it is constructed with hindsight, it does not involve financial risk, and no
backtest can fully account for the effect of real market conditions on a live strategy.

## Your responsibility

Any decision you make is **your own**. You are responsible for your own due diligence, for
complying with the laws and regulations applicable to you, and for determining whether any use
of this data is appropriate for your circumstances. If you need advice, consult a qualified,
appropriately licensed professional.
